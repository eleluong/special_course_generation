## Assessment Planning

### Assessment Overview
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Assessment Philosophy** | Mixed formative and summative approach to support continuous learning and final mastery |
| **Grading Scale** | 100‑point scale (converted to percentages) |
| **Objective Mapping** | All assessments directly map to the Primary and Secondary learning objectives listed in the course analysis |
| **Feedback Timeline** | Formative feedback within 48 hours; summative feedback within 5 business days |

### Module 1: Marketing Fundamentals

#### Formative Assessments
| Assessment | Format | Duration | Objectives Measured | Weight |
|------------|--------|----------|-------------------|--------|
| 4Ps Quick Quiz | Multiple‑choice quiz | 10 min | Objective 1: Identify and justify the four Ps | 5 % |
| Case Study Discussion | Online discussion forum | 15 min | Secondary Objective 3: Communication of marketing insights | 5 % |

#### Summative Assessments
| Assessment | Format | Weight | Rubric Criteria |
|------------|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Scenario‑Based 4Ps Quiz | SCORM quiz with scenario analysis | 40 % | • **Accuracy**: Correct identification of all 4Ps (≥ 80 %)<br>• **Justification**: Clear, evidence‑based explanation (≥ 80 %)<br>• **Application**: Relevance to the case study (≥ 80 %) |

### Module 2: Market Research & Digital Campaign

#### Formative Assessments
| Assessment | Format | Duration | Objectives Measured | Weight |
|------------|--------|----------|-------------------|--------|
| Survey Prototype Workshop | Interactive lab | 20 min | Primary Objective 2: Survey design | 10 % |
| Data Analysis Lab | Hands‑on spreadsheet exercise | 20 min | Secondary Objective 1: Data literacy | 10 % |

#### Summative Assessments
| Assessment | Format | Weight | Rubric Criteria |
|------------|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Digital Marketing Campaign Plan | Project submission (brief, calendar, KPI dashboard) | 40 % | • **Creativity**: Innovative use of platform features (≥ 80 %)<br>• **Feasibility**: Realistic timeline & resources (≥ 80 %)<br>• **Metrics**: Clear KPIs aligned to objectives (≥ 80 %) |

### Final Assessment
| Component | Description | Weight | Evaluation Criteria |
|-----------|-------------|--------|---------------------|
| **Capstone Project** | Integrated marketing plan for a new product, including 4Ps, research strategy, and digital campaign | 30 % | Demonstrates mastery of all Primary Objectives; rubric scores ≥ 80 % |
| **Comprehensive Evaluation** | 5‑minute reflective video and written summary linking course concepts to workplace application | 10 % | Clarity, depth of reflection, and actionable insights (≥ 80 %) |
| **Total Final Assessment** | | 40 % | Combined score of Capstone Project and Comprehensive Evaluation |
