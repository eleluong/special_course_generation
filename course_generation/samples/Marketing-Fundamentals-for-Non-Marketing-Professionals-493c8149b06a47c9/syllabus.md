## Course Syllabus

### Course Overview
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Duration** | 6 weeks (48 hours total) |
| **Format** | Online self‑paced with weekly live Q&A |
| **Prerequisites** | Basic spreadsheet proficiency (Excel/Sheets) |
| **Target Audience** | Mid‑level operations, finance, HR, and product managers with no formal marketing education |

### Module Structure

| Module | Title | Duration | Learning Outcomes | Prerequisites |
|--------|-------|----------|-------------------|---------------|
| 1 | Foundations of Marketing for Non‑Marketers | 2 weeks | • Identify the 4Ps and explain their relevance to cross‑functional roles<br>• Define key marketing terminology and concepts<br>• Apply marketing logic to evaluate a product/service case | None |
| 2 | Market Research & Data Analytics | 2 weeks | • Critique primary and secondary market‑research techniques<br>• Design a survey instrument for a target segment<br>• Interpret basic market data visualizations using Excel and Tableau | Completion of Module 1, Basic spreadsheet proficiency |
| 3 | Digital Marketing & Campaign Planning | 2 weeks | • Develop a digital‑marketing campaign plan using a chosen social‑media platform<br>• Create a content calendar and KPI dashboard<br>• Present marketing insights to non‑marketing stakeholders | Completion of Modules 1 & 2 |

### Course Schedule
| Timeframe | Coverage | Milestones |
|-----------|----------|------------|
| Week 1‑2 | Foundations of Marketing | • Glossary of marketing terms<br>• 4Ps quiz (≥ 80 %)<br>• Case analysis write‑up |
| Week 3‑4 | Market Research & Data Analytics | • Survey prototype submission (rubric ≥ 4/5)<br>• Data‑analysis lab (peer review) |
| Week 5‑6 | Digital Marketing & Campaign Planning | • Campaign brief, content calendar, KPI dashboard<br>• 5‑minute presentation (rubric ≥ 4/5) |
