### Presentation Guidelines
| Aspect | Specification |
|--------|---------------|
| **Slide Design** | Use a clean, professional template with consistent color palette and typography; incorporate brand‑aligned icons and high‑contrast text. |
| **Animation/Transitions** | Apply subtle fade‑in for bullet points and slide‑level transitions (e.g., dissolve) to maintain focus; avoid distracting motion effects. |
| **Accessibility** | Font size ≥ 24 pt for body text, high‑contrast color scheme (≥ 4.5:1), alt text for all images, captions for videos, keyboard‑navigable interactive elements. |
| **Time per Slide** | 2–3 minutes per slide, allowing 5–6 minutes for discussion or Q&A after each module. |
