### Risk Assessment
| Risk Category | Potential Issues | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|---------------|------------------|-------------|--------|-------------------|
| **Resource Availability** | SME unavailable during key weeks | Medium | High | Schedule buffer weeks; assign backup SME |
| **Technical Challenges** | LMS integration errors, tool incompatibilities | Low | Medium | Conduct pre‑deployment testing; use vendor support |
| **Content Quality** | Outdated case studies, inaccurate data | Low | Medium | Review all materials against 2025 sources; update quarterly |
| **Learner Engagement** | Low completion rates, passive participation | Medium | High | Incorporate micro‑learning, interactive labs, gamified quizzes |
| **Accessibility** | Non‑compliant content, missing captions | Low | Medium | WCAG 2.1 AA audit; provide transcripts and alt text |
| **Budget Overruns** | Unexpected license costs, overtime | Medium | High | Monitor spend monthly; adjust scope if needed |
| **Timeline Delays** | Delayed procurement, resource conflicts | Medium | High | Maintain Gantt chart, regular status meetings |
