## Assessment Package

### Blueprint
| Objective | Item Type | Count | Cognitive Level | Notes |

### Questions

1. **Type:** MCQ  
   **Stem:** Which sequence best represents the stages of a social media campaign from planning to optimization?  
   **Options:**  
   - A) Content Creation → Scheduling → Budget Allocation → Reporting  
   - B) Goal Definition → Audience Research → Platform Selection → Content Calendar → KPI Setup → A/B Testing → Reporting  
   - C) Budget Allocation → Content Calendar → Audience Research → Goal Definition → Reporting  
   - D) Audience Research → Goal Definition → Content Creation → Reporting → Optimization  
   **Answer:** B  
   **Rationale:** Option B correctly lists the logical flow: start with clear goals, research the audience, choose the platform, build a content calendar, set KPIs, test variations, and finally report findings. Options A, C, and D omit critical steps or present them in the wrong order.  
   **Alignment:** LO1  

2. **Type:** Short Answer  
   **Prompt:** Draft a 4‑week content calendar for a new eco‑friendly product on Instagram, including post types, timing, and three KPIs you would track.  
   **Expected Key Points:**  
   - **Week 1:**  
     - Mon – Image post (product spotlight) – 10 am – KPI: Reach  
     - Wed – Reel (how‑to use) – 2 pm – KPI: Engagement rate  
     - Fri – Carousel (customer testimonials) – 6 pm – KPI: Click‑through rate  
   - **Week 2:**  
     - Mon – Story (poll) – 9 am – KPI: Story completion rate  
     - Wed – Image (sustainability facts) – 1 pm – KPI: Reach  
     - Fri – Reel (behind‑the‑scenes) – 5 pm – KPI: Engagement rate  
   - **Week 3:**  
     - Mon – Image (limited‑edition teaser) – 11 am – KPI: Reach  
     - Wed – Carousel (product comparison) – 3 pm – KPI: Click‑through rate  
     - Fri – Story (Q&A) – 7 pm – KPI: Story completion rate  
   - **Week 4:**  
     - Mon – Reel (launch announcement) – 12 pm – KPI: Engagement rate  
     - Wed – Image (user‑generated content) – 4 pm – KPI: Reach  
     - Fri – Carousel (call‑to‑action) – 8 pm – KPI: Click‑through rate  
   **Alignment:** LO2  

3. **Type:** Practical Task  
   **Task:** Create a comprehensive digital marketing campaign plan that includes:  
   1. Platform selection rationale (brief justification).  
   2. A 4‑week content calendar (as in Q2).  
   3. A KPI dashboard template in Google Sheets (with formulas for reach, engagement, CTR, CPM, and ROI).  
   4. A 5‑minute stakeholder presentation video (recorded screen‑share + voice‑over).  
   **Submission:**  
   - PDF of the campaign plan (platform rationale, calendar, KPI definitions).  
   - Google Sheets link to the KPI dashboard (shared with read‑only access).  
   - MP4 video file of the presentation (≤ 5 min).  
   **Rubric:**  
   | Criterion | Weight |
   |-----------|--------|
   | Strategy Alignment (relevance to objectives & target audience) | 30 % |
   | Content Calendar (coherence, timing, variety) | 25 % |
   | KPI Dashboard (completeness, accuracy, usability) | 20 % |
   | Presentation (clarity, stakeholder focus, storytelling) | 25 % |
   **Alignment:** LO3
