## Presentation Plan

### Slide List
1. **Title:** Digital Landscape Overview – Channels, User Journey, Key Metrics  
   - **Bullets:**  
     - Multichannel ecosystem: paid, owned, earned  
     - Typical user journey: awareness → consideration → conversion → advocacy  
     - Key performance metrics: reach, engagement, conversion, ROI  
   - **Visual/Diagram:**  
     Channel funnel overlayed with a user journey map  
   - **Speaker Notes:**  
     Explain how each channel feeds into the journey; highlight the importance of aligning metrics with business goals  

2. **Title:** Social Media Campaign Blueprint – Platform Selection, Content Calendar, Engagement Tracking  
   - **Bullets:**  
     - Choose platform based on audience demographics & platform strengths  
     - Build a 4‑week content calendar with mix of formats (image, video, carousel)  
     - Track engagement (likes, comments, shares, saves) and adjust timing  
   - **Visual/Diagram:**  
     Sample content calendar grid + engagement flowchart  
   - **Speaker Notes:**  
     Walk through a real‑world example (eco‑friendly product on Instagram); discuss AI‑driven scheduling and privacy‑compliant data collection  

3. **Title:** Measuring Success & Optimization – KPI Dashboard, A/B Testing, Continuous Improvement Loop  
   - **Bullets:**  
     - Create KPI dashboard with reach, CTR, CPC, CPM, conversion rate, LTV  
     - Run A/B tests on creative & copy to identify top performers  
     - Iterate using a continuous improvement cycle (measure → analyse → optimise → repeat)  
   - **Visual/Diagram:**  
     KPI dashboard mock‑up + A/B testing diagram  
   - **Speaker Notes:**  
     Demonstrate using Google Sheets template; explain how to interpret results and feed back into strategy  

### Visual Guidelines
- **Color/Contrast:**  
  Use high‑contrast palette (dark text on light background); WCAG 2.1 AA minimum 4.5:1 for body text  
- **Font/Size:**  
  Title 36 pt, Body 24 pt, Bullet 24 pt; sans‑serif (Calibri or Arial)  
- **Diagram Conventions:**  
  Use clear icons, labels, and consistent color coding; avoid color‑only cues  

### Timing Plan
- **Total Slides:** 3  
- **Estimated Duration:** 30 minutes (10 min per slide + 5 min Q&A)  
- **Pace Notes:**  
  Pause after each slide to ask a quick reflection question; switch to interactive poll during slide 2; end with a 5‑min live Q&A
